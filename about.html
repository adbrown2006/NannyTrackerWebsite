About NannyTracker

About the App

NannyTracker helps nannies and families stay organized and improve communication around childcare responsibilities. The app allows users to track schedules, log hours worked, manage daily tasks, record mileage, and estimate compensation based on user-entered information. By keeping all important details in one central location, NannyTracker simplifies coordination and reduces misunderstandings, helping caregivers and families stay aligned.

The app is designed to be an easy-to-use organizational and record-keeping tool. It does not provide legal, payroll, tax, or employment services. Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of information entered and compliance with applicable childcare and employment laws. By providing clear, accurate tracking, NannyTracker helps parents and caregivers make informed decisions and maintain transparency.

About Us

NannyTracker was created to address the challenges families and nannies face in managing schedules, hours worked, compensation, and communication. Our goal is to simplify and automate these processes while providing an intuitive, reliable platform that supports both caregivers and families.

Our mission is to reduce confusion, improve transparency, and promote smoother working relationships between families and the nannies they trust. By offering tools to log hours, track mileage, calculate compensation, and monitor daily childcare activities, NannyTracker ensures that both parents and caregivers can stay organized and confident in their responsibilities.

We believe that accurate record-keeping and clear communication are essential to fostering trust and accountability in childcare relationships. NannyTracker is designed to help families and caregivers work together efficiently, avoid misunderstandings, and maintain a professional approach to childcare management.

Ultimately, NannyTracker strives to provide a reliable, easy-to-use platform that supports better organization, improved communication, and more confidence for both families and caregivers. By keeping childcare and work-related information centralized, our app empowers users to focus on what matters most: providing high-quality care for children while maintaining a clear understanding of work and compensation.
