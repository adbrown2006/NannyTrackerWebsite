Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. NannyTracker uses secure storage and encryption to protect user information.

Can multiple caregivers use the app?

Yes. Parents and caregivers can share access and stay synced.

Does NannyTracker collect data from children?

No. NannyTracker is designed for parents and caregivers. Any child-related data is entered by adults.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You may request data deletion through our Contact Page.

How can I get support?

You can reach us through our Contact Page.

Who is NannyTracker designed for?

NannyTracker is designed for parents, guardians, nannies, babysitters, and other authorized caregivers who want to track childcare activities, routines, and important daily events. The app helps families stay organized and ensures everyone caring for a child stays informed and connected.

Is NannyTracker free to use?

Yes. NannyTracker is completely free to download and use.

What kind of activities can I track?

NannyTracker allows users to track childcare needs and work-related information, including children’s daily care needs, nanny work hours, hourly rates, total revenue earned or owed each week, qualifying mileage driven for compensation, and nanny scheduling. The app helps maintain accurate records and simplifies communication between caregivers and families.

What happens if I change or lose my device?

If you change or lose your device, your data remains securely stored in your account. You can sign in again on a new device to regain access to your childcare tracking information. If you experience any issues accessing your account, you can contact support for assistance.

How do I delete my account or data?

Users can request account or data deletion at any time. You can submit a deletion request through the Contact page or by contacting support directly. Once your request is verified, your data will be permanently removed in accordance with applicable privacy laws and platform requirements.

Is my information shared with third parties?

NannyTracker does not sell personal information. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers that help operate the app (such as hosting or technical services), and only when necessary to provide the service. Full details about data handling are available in the Privacy Policy.
