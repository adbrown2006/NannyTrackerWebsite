Features

Track Childcare Needs

NannyTracker allows parents and caregivers to record and monitor essential childcare activities. This includes tracking children’s feeding schedules, naps, playtime, and other daily routines. By keeping all childcare information in one place, families and nannies can stay informed and coordinate care more efficiently.

Log Hours Worked

Caregivers can log the hours they work for each family, helping both parties keep accurate records of time spent. NannyTracker automatically organizes and summarizes hours worked, making it easy to track schedules and ensure everyone is on the same page.

Manage Daily Tasks

Users can create and manage checklists of daily tasks and responsibilities. Whether it’s chores, appointments, or special instructions for the child, NannyTracker ensures important details are visible and never overlooked.

Track Mileage and Compensation

NannyTracker allows users to record mileage that qualifies for reimbursement and calculate compensation based on hours worked and agreed-upon rates. Total revenue earned, amounts owed, and other financial information can be tracked weekly, providing transparency for both families and caregivers.

Schedule Coordination

The app includes tools to manage and coordinate schedules between nannies and families. Users can set recurring work hours, view upcoming appointments, and adjust schedules as needed. This reduces confusion and ensures that childcare coverage is consistent and reliable.

Record-Keeping and Organization

All childcare and work-related information is stored securely within the app, providing a single, organized place for notes, logs, and records. NannyTracker helps reduce misunderstandings and disputes by keeping accurate, accessible documentation of all activities and compensation details.

Easy Communication

By centralizing information, NannyTracker improves communication between parents and caregivers. Users can easily share updates, track progress, and coordinate care without relying on multiple tools or manual tracking methods.

Professional and Responsible Tool

NannyTracker is designed as an organizational and record-keeping tool. It does not provide legal, payroll, tax, or employment services. Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of information entered and for complying with applicable childcare and employment laws. The app focuses on simplifying coordination, improving transparency, and supporting smooth working relationships.
